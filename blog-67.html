IS IT STILL MUSIC?

  • Pranita Abbireddy
  • Class of 2025

Imagine coming across a composition, listening to every beat, and being completely mesmerized, only to find Artificial Intelligence generated it. Surprised? Well, this is what an AI music generator does to you. Created by a combination of computer scientists, musicians, and engineers, this technology combines knowledge and techniques from various areas, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, music theory, and signal processing.

AI music generators are computer programs that use artificial intelligence to create music. They can generate music in various genres and styles using algorithms and machine learning models trained on existing music data. Some allow users to input specific parameters, such as tempo, key, and chord progression, to guide the generated music. The output produced by the generator can range from simple melodies to complex compositions. It is capable of generating entire pieces of music from scratch. It makes one wonder if AI can replace humans in making music.

“The key to artificial intelligence has always been the representation.”

The answer varies depending on who you ask. Some believe that it has the potential to democratize the music creation process and increase accessibility to music production tools. On the other hand, others worry that this technology will decrease the value placed on human musicianship and creativity. Humans rely on their passion and emotions to make music that can help people express themselves or teleport them into a world that the artist constructed. In contrast, AI music generators rely on past data to do the same.

AI music generation has the potential to both disrupt and complement traditional music production processes. For example, it can quickly generate background music for commercials or video games or create new versions of existing songs. Additionally, it can be used in the music education industry to help students learn about music theory and composition.

AI music generators can help people who want to create music in different ways :

  1. 1.Inspiration: They can be used as a source of inspiration for musicians, providing new ideas and concepts to explore in their compositions.
  2. 2.Time-saving: Can generate simple pieces of music quickly and efficiently, freeing up more time for musicians to focus on more creative aspects of music production.
  3. 3.Accessibility: AI music generators can make music production tools more accessible to individuals who may not have extensive musical training or experience with music production software.
  4. 4.Music theory education: Some generators are designed to help educate individuals about music theory and composition, providing an interactive and engaging learning experience.
  5. 5.Automation: They can automate certain aspects of the music production process, such as drum programming or melody generation, allowing musicians to focus on more creative tasks.

Creating music can be a therapeutic activity for some individuals. AI music generators could contribute to this by providing individuals with an accessible and engaging way to compose music and express themselves. However, it does not guarantee the quality and creativity of music as it depends on the specific AI system and the training data used. Once you notice that an AI produced the music, it all feels alien and mechanical. As time progresses, the AI music generator will evolve though nothing can supersede the need for musical skills, creativity, and experience.
