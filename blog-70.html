Life is a Journey

  • Ranga Vaishnavi
  • Class of 2025

Life is a journey. The first journey begins when you open your eyes to the world for the first time. It is full of pleasant experiences and other less pleasant ones. You will discover the good and bad in this journey. Here is a small trip with my friend I wanted to share with you.

Last summer my best friend and I spontaneously decided to extend the holidays by escaping to Europe. This is my first ever independent trip. Europe was amazing in a way that you get to know about different cultures, traditions, languages, and food. I enjoyed learning new things in Europe. Every day I was fascinated by various cultures and history. Utmost, I relished freedom and loved the diversity of all. Through this trip, I met a lot of amazing people with whom I have memories. Memories are priceless treasures that we cherish forever in our hearts.

Life is all about experiences. Traveling is a unique experience and it’s the best way to unplug the pushes and pulls in life. Life is a journey filled with unexpected miracles. Choose the right way to be successful in your life as it is not a destination.

“Know more about yourself and make yourself happier by traveling.”

In conclusion, explore yourself and experience new challenges and learn something new every day.
